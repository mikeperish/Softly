# Softly — Antistress & Focus

A pocket antistress companion for restless minds. Interactive fidgets with haptic feedback, ambient sounds, and a gentle Pomodoro timer — all designed for people with ADHD and anxiety.

## Features

**Pop** — Pixel-art bubble wrap with satisfying pops. Three unique silhouettes to discover. Pop them all and watch the next one appear.

**Sound** — Shape ambient noise with your finger. White, Brown, and Grey noise with real-time tone and volume control. Beautiful ripple effects follow every touch.

**Cube** — An infinite digital fidget cube. Swipe 16 tiles in any direction to flip and reveal new colors with smooth 3D animations.

**Focus** — A Pomodoro timer built for ADHD. Standard (25 min) and ADHD-friendly (15 min) modes. Tracks sessions, streaks, and focus minutes. Works in the background while you fidget.

**Physics** — A spinning top powered by your phone's gyroscope. Swipe to spin, tilt to roll. Two mesmerizing patterns with realistic wall-bounce physics.

## Design

- Dark, soothing interface (#0A0A0F)
- Instant haptic feedback on every interaction
- No ads, no distractions
- Sound plays across tabs
- Minimal UI — zero clutter

## Tech Stack

- SwiftUI, iOS 17+
- SpriteKit & CoreMotion
- AVAudioEngine for procedural audio
- RevenueCat for subscriptions
- Firebase Crashlytics

## Privacy

Softly does not collect, store, or share any personal data. All app data (timer stats, preferences) is stored locally on your device.
